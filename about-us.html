“Discover your Passion”

A growing amount of the youth are discontent with the current education system.

Project Arch is our solution to this complex and fundamental problem.

Instead of the current system that’s both outdated and boring, students will now be able to freely pick and choose what they want to learn, from a wide range of dynamic, project- and practical-based tutorials.

Made by students, for students.
